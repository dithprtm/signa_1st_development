Read Write Listen

Welcome to our website, signa is a design concept which built for developing personal creativity based on what we read, write and listen. We talk a lot about music, movie, book, design, architecture and culture.

signa?

Read

Through reading, individuals gain knowledge, insights, and inspiration from books, articles, and various written materials. This exposure helps in understanding different viewpoints, cultures, and experiences, serving as a valuable source of inspiration for creative projects.

Write

Writing serves as a means of expression and exploration, allowing individuals to articulate their thoughts, ideas, and emotions. Through writing, people can document their experiences, develop narratives, and communicate unique perspectives, thus facilitating self-reflection and creative exploration.

Listen

Listening involves engaging with auditory content such as music, podcasts, speeches, or conversations. Just like reading and writing, listening stimulates creativity by evoking emotions, inspiring new ideas, and providing opportunities to learn from others.
